Renouncing the Contract:

Renouncing the contract refers to a critical step in making your cryptocurrency project fully decentralized. When you renounce a contract, it means that you, as the project's developer or creator, relinquish control over the smart contract governing the token.

Security

Locking liquidity and renouncing the contract are significant steps in the development of a cryptocurrency project like Redmoon. Here's an explanation of what these actions mean: Lock liquidity refers to securing a portion of your cryptocurrency's tokens and pairing them with an equivalent value in another cryptocurrency, typically on a decentralized exchange (DEX). This process helps ensure that there is enough liquidity in the market to facilitate trading of your token, Redmon liquidity is 100% LOCKED.
